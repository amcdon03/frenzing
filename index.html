Watashitachi Friends

A foreign language penfriend site.

  • Make Friends

    Get to know someone in another language to hear about their country and their lifestyle.

  • Socialise with Technology

    Sharing chat about soap operas, music and video clips and so on is easy if you sling your friend a link. This is a great way to get a better understanding of what's going on in their world.

  • Have Fun Learning

    Writing is one of the hardest skills when learning a new language, but if you write about what matters to you and share it with a friend, they can help you get much better.

About Watashitachi

Watashitachi Friends (^_^)/

Watashitachi is a foreign language penfriend site. Students can connect with each other in different countries and share their interests, thoughts and ideas.

Watashitachi helps teachers to develop their language skills through their communication with students abroad.

Login

Let's chat some more!

  • Make Friends

    Get to know someone in another language to hear about their country and their lifestyle.

  • Socialise with Technology

    Sharing chat about soap operas, music and video clips and so on is easy if you sling sling your friend a link. This is a great way to get a better understanding of what's going on in their world.

  • Have Fun Learning

    Writing is one of the hardest skills when learning a new language, but if you write about what matters to you and share it with a friend, they can help you get much better.
